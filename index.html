Dashboard



With TaskBliss, you have a centralized hub where you can view and manage all your tasks effortlessly. This feature provides a comprehensive dashboard that displays every task and project at a glance, allowing you to:

  • Organize and Prioritize: Easily categorize tasks based on their importance and urgency. You can sort tasks into various groups, such as pending, in-progress, and completed, making it simpler to focus on what needs attention first.
  • Track Progress: Monitor the status of each task with visual indicators and progress bars. See how much work is left and keep track of milestones to ensure you're on target.
  • Manage Deadlines: View all upcoming deadlines and due dates in one place. This helps you plan your work better and avoid missing critical deadlines.
  • Update and Edit: Quickly make changes to tasks, update details, or adjust deadlines directly from the dashboard. This ensures that your task list remains accurate and up-to-date.
  • Collaborate Effectively: Share your task view with team members to keep everyone informed. Assign tasks to different members, check their progress, and facilitate seamless communication.

Sort Tasks



TaskBliss provides a powerful task sorting feature that allows you to efficiently organize and manage your tasks based on their priority levels. This functionality enhances your productivity by ensuring you focus on what’s most important and manage your workload more effectively. Here’s how it works:

  • Ascending Priority Sorting: Organize your tasks so that those with the lowest priority appear at the top. This view is useful for managing tasks that are less urgent or have lower importance, allowing you to systematically address lower-priority items once higher-priority tasks are completed.
  • Descending Priority Sorting: Alternatively, you can sort tasks with the highest priority at the top. This view highlights the most critical tasks first, helping you to quickly focus on what needs immediate attention and ensure that high-priority items are completed on time.
  • Customizable Filters: Tailor the sorting options to fit your workflow. Apply filters to view tasks based on specific criteria such as deadlines, tags, or categories, in addition to priority levels.
  • Visual Indicators: Utilize visual cues like color-coded labels or icons to easily identify task priorities. This feature enhances clarity and helps you quickly distinguish between tasks of varying importance.
